Start CAMSS Incompatibility Assessment

The goal of the CAMSS incompatibility assessment is to determine whether integrating a specification/standard into an existing environment (AS-IS environment) could potentially disrupt the environment's interoperability with other solutions it exchanges data and information with. Typically, new specifications are integrated into the AS-IS Environment to implement new digital business capabilities or to adapt the environment to meet new interoperability requirements. The result of this integration is the TO-BE Environment (the desired target environment).
